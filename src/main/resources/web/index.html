Models

Four versions are available depending on what inputs you have.

Grass activity

ECOSYS_12_02_2018_Grass_Activity.eco
  • Total deposition,
  • Activity in grass,
  • Yield of grass (optional),
  • Amount of rainfall

Wet deposition

ECOSYS_12_02_2018_Wet_Deposition.eco
  • Activity concentration in air,
  • Wet deposition,
  • Amount of rainfall

Total deposition

ECOSYS_13_02_2018_Total_Deposition.eco
  • Activity concentration in air,
  • total deposition (onto bare soil/pasture/lawn),
  • amount of rainfall
